At Brew Haven Coffee, we value customer satisfaction and strive to ensure that every order meets our quality standards. To maintain transparency and trust, we have established the following refund policy:
- Customers are eligible for a refund if the received order is incorrect or if an error occurs on our part.
- Refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of purchase.
- Refunds are only applicable for transactions made through official Brew Haven stores or the Brew Haven mobile application.
- Once a refund request is approved, the refund process may take 3–5 business days to complete.
- To request a refund, customers may contact us with relevant order details.

This policy is designed to ensure a fair and efficient resolution process while maintaining Brew Haven Coffee’s commitment to quality and service excellence.
