Brew Haven Coffee offers a customer loyalty program called “Brew Points”, designed to reward our loyal guests for every purchase.

Program Details:
- Customers earn 1 Brew Point for every Rp10,000 spent on eligible purchases.
- 10 Brew Points can be redeemed for one free regular-sized beverage of choice.
- Members also receive exclusive birthday promotions and early access to new menu items before their public release.
- Membership is free, and customers can register through the Brew Haven mobile app or directly at the in-store cashier.

The Brew Points program aims to enhance customer engagement and appreciation by providing tangible rewards and exclusive experiences for our valued patrons.
